What is Tears of the Kingdom?

"The Legend of Zelda: Tears of the Kingdom is the upcoming twentieth main installment in The Legend of Zelda series. The game is scheduled to be released on May 12, 2023 on the Nintendo Switch. Development of the game began in 2017 after the production for Breath of the Wild concluded. During the E3 2019 Nintendo Direct, it was announced that it would be a sequel to Breath of the Wild. The idea of creating a direct sequel came from the fact that Eiji Aonuma, producer of Breath of the Wild, wanted to revisit the same Hyrule while incorporating a new story and gameplay mechanics. Initially, new ideas were thought of for DLC but evolved into a new game when too many were gathered." - Zelda Wiki
